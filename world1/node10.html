You walk over and pickup the one by the chair. It's quite heavy to the touch, and looks like that device the old man in the meadow had. Its surface is smooth, but there's an inscription: "tiny".

"Tiny?", you say out loud, and suddenly that feeling of being compressed is back, and you are blacking out...

"Help!" you yell, but there is no answer. "HEEEEEEEEEEEELLLLLLLLLPPPPPPP!" you scream. You are in way over your head.
